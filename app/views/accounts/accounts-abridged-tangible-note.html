Cost at 1 April 2016

The cost of your tangible assets at the start of this accounting period.

Additions

The cost of your tangible assets acquired during this accounting period.

Disposals

The cost of tangible assets disposed of in this acccounting period.

Revaluations

Valuation of any assets revalued during this accounting period.

If the revaluation reduces the value of the asset, then enter a negative figure.

Transfers

The cost or valuation of any tangible assets that you've transferred during this accounting period. If the asset is being transferred out, enter a negative figure.

Cost at 31 March 2017

Calculation: 'Cost at 1 April 2016' plus 'additions', plus 'revaluations' plus 'transfers' minus 'disposals'.

Depreciation Depreciation is the reduction in value of an asset over its useful lifetime.

Depreciation at 1 April 2016

The reduction in your tangible assets at the start of this accounting period.

Charge for year

The reduction in tangible asset values during this accounting period.

On disposals

Total depreciation for all tangible assets disposed of in this accounting period.

Other adjustments

Any other depreciation adjustment on tangible assets.

Depreciation at 31 March 2017

Calculation: 'Depreciation at 1 April 2016' plus 'Charge for year' plus 'Other adjustments' minus 'On disposals'.

Net book value Net book value is the original asset cost minus depreciation.

Net book value at 31 March 2017

Calculation: 'Cost at 31 March 2017' minus 'Depreciation at 31 March 2017'.

Net book value at 31 March 2016

Calculation: 'Cost at 1 April 2016' minus 'Depreciation at 1 April 2016'.
