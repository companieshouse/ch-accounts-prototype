Gross profit or loss

Distribution costs

Costs like packaging, transporting or storing the goods or services sold.

Administrative expenses

'Intangible assets' plus 'tangible assets'.

Operating profit (loss)

'Gross profit' minus 'distribution costs', minus 'administrative expenses', plus 'other operating income'.

Interest receivable and similar income

Interest earned or owed to the company eg bank interest, interest from loans.

Profit (loss) on ordinary activities before taxation

'Operating profit' plus 'interest receivable', minus 'interest payable'.

Tax on profit or loss on ordinary activities

Taxable income is 'profit or loss on ordinary activities before tax' minus any reliefs due. You can work out the Corporation Tax due on taxable income using the latest rates.

Profit (loss) for the financial year

'Profit or loss on ordinary activities before tax' minus 'tax on profit or loss ordinary activities'.
