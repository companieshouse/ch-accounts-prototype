Not sure if your company was dormant?

Companies House considers your company to be dormant if it had no transactions in the financial year that you'd be expected to include in your accounts.

Your company would still be dormant if you made the following transactions (you wouldn't include these in your accounts):

  • filing fees paid to Companies House
  • penalties for filing the accounts late
  • money paid for shares when the company was incorporated
