File full accounts to Companies House and HMRC

Important information

  • you will be directed to HMRC's online services to use the joint filing service
  • you will need Government Gateway credentials to access HMRC's online services
  • newly requested Government Gateway credentials are issued by post, so allow a few days for receipt

Key accounts once - use twice

HMRC and Companies House are providing a service that you can use to submit audit exempt full accounts or micro-entity accounts to both HMRC and Companies House (with abbreviated options for filing to Companies House). To learn more read how joint filing works.

If you joint file your accounts, Companies House will still send you an email to confirm receipt and another to confirm if they have been accepted or rejected.

To check if the service is suitable for your company, read accounts exceptions.
