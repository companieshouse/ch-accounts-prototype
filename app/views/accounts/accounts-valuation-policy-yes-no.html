Do your prepared accounts include a 'valuation information' policy?

What's this?Valuation information policy

If you have stocks or work in progress, this policy describes how they are valued. Stocks should be valued at the lower of either cost or ‘net realisable value’ (the realistic resale value minus the cost of selling it).

For example, 'Stocks and work in progress are valued at the lower of cost and net realisable value, after making allowances for obsolete and slow-moving items. Cost includes all direct expenditure and overheads'.
