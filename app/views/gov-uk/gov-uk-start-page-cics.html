File Community Interest Company report and small full annual accounts with Companies House

Use this service to file your Community Interest Company report and small full annual accounts with Companies House.

Before you start

You'll need:

  • to prepare your Community Interest Company report
  • to prepare your small full accounts
  • to round figures to whole numbers, with income round down and spending round up
  • to have your accounts approved by the director or board of directors
  • a £15 payment by MasterCard, Visa, Electron, Maestro or Delta debit or credit card

Read guidance on what your annual accounts should include.

How to register

You’ll need a Companies House password and authentication code.

If you don’t have these, you'll be able to register online after you've chosen which type of accounts you want to file.

Get help

Ask us for help if you:

  • don’t have a computer or internet access
  • want to use the online service but need some help
