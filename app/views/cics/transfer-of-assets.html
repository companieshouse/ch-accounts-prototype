What's this?

This generally means any transfer of the CIC's assets that have not reached full market value during this financial year. For example, if an asset was valued at £1,000 and the CIC sold it for less (say £500), you should declare why. This also applies if the CIC gave any assets to another CIC or a charity.

For more details, read our guidance (PDF 297kb) or speak to your accountant.
