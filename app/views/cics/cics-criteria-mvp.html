You can file small full accounts here if:

  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to
  • your balance sheet doesn’t have 'intangible assets', 'fixed asset investments', 'debtors', 'creditors - amounts falling after one year', or 'revaluation reserve'
  • you only need to include balance sheet notes for 'tangible assets' and 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year'

Your CIC must also meet at least 2 of the following:

  • turnover is not over £10.2 million
  • balance sheet total is not over £5.1 million
  • average number of employees is not over 50
