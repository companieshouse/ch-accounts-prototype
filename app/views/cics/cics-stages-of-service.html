File CIC annual report and accounts

Use this service to file a simplified CIC annual report and full company accounts

If you can't finish your filing in one session, you can come back later to continue. You'll need to go to your filings.

There are 3 stages to this service:

  1. 1. CIC annual report

    • General description of the company's activities and impact
    • Consultation with stakeholders
    • Directors' renumeration
    • Transfers of assets other than for full consideration
    • Check your answers
    • Approve your CIC annual report

  2. 2. Full accounts

    • Confirm your company account dates
    • Balance sheet
    • Balance sheet statements
    • Balance sheet notes
    • Check your answers
    • Approve your full accounts

  3. 3. Payment

    • Pay and submit
