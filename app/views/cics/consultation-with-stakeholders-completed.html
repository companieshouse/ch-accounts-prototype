Has there been a consultation with stakeholders?

A stakeholder is a party that has an interest in the CIC and can either affect or be affected by its business and activities.

If there's been any consultation with stakeholders, you should mention:

  • who the company's stakeholders are
  • how the stakeholders have been consulted
  • any action the company has taken in response to feedback from its consultations
