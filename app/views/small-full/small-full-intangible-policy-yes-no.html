Balance sheet notes Do your prepared accounts include an 'intangible fixed assets amortisation' policy?

If you have intangible (non-physical) fixed assets, this policy describes how the amortisation (reduction in value) of each asset is calculated over its expected useful lifetime. Amortisation is similar to the depreciation value of a tangible (physical) asset.

For example, 'Annual amortisation of goodwill is calculated at 25% to write off the cost of the asset, minus its residual value, over its estimated useful lifetime'.
