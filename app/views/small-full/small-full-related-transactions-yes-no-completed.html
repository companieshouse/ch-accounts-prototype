Do your prepared accounts include a 'related party transactions' note?

What's this?Fixed assets investments

Use this note to record transactions between related parties, for example, selling a company asset for more or less than market value or paying them rent or consultancy fees. Parties are 'related' when one has control or significant influence over the other, for example directors and investors (or close family members of either).
