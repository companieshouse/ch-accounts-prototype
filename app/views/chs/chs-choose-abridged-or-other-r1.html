File abridged accounts

Criteria

Check your prepared accounts to see if you meet the criteria.

You can file abridged accounts here if:

  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to
  • your balance sheet doesn’t have 'fixed asset investments'
  • you don't have to add notes for 'revaluation, reserve', 'fixed assets investments, 'debtors', 'loans to directors', or 'related party transactions'

Your company must also meet at least 2 of the following:

  • turnover is not over £10.2 million
  • balance sheet total is not over £5.1 million
  • average number of employees is not over 50
