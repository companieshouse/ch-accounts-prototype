Try the new abridged accounts filing

There have been changes in law, which means you can’t file abbreviated accounts for accounting periods starting on or after 1 January 2016.

We’re launching a new private beta service which would allow you to file abridged accounts.

More information

You can only try the new service if:

  • your accounting period starts on or after 1 January 2016
  • your balance sheet doesn’t include 'intangible assets', 'fixed asset investments' or 'revaluation reserve'
  • you just need to supply an 'accounting policies' note (and, if needed, a 'tangible assets' note and/or a 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year’ note)
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to

Register your interest

If you meet the criteria and would like to try the new service, send an email to privatebeta@companieshouse.gov.uk and we’ll get back to you.
